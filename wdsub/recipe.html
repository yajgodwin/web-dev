Oreo Red Velvet Cake Mix Cookies

  1. Ingredients

    • Red Velevet Cake Mix
    • 1/3 of a cup of Canola Oil
    • 1 egg (room temperature)
    • Small Chocolate Chips (Optional)
    • Oreos

  2. Materials

    1. Medium Bowl
    2. Mixing Spoon
    3. Baking Sheet
    4. Parchment Paper
    5. Gloves

  3. Prep Work

    • Preheat the oven to 375 degress
    • Take your egg out of the fridge
    • Put your parchment paper on your baking sheet

  4. Make the Batter

    1. Put the cake mix in the bowl
    2. Put the egg and oil in the bowl with the cake mix
    3. Stir until a dough-like batter has formed
    4. Add in your chocolate chips into your batter (As much as you want)

  5. Assemble

    1. Take your cookie dough and mold it onto the oreo until the oreo is completely covered
    2. Place your cookie onto the baking sheet
    3. Repeat this process until you run out of cookie dough

  6. Bake

    1. Place your cookies into the oven
    2. Let the cookies bake for 7 to 9 minutes
    3. Once they are done, take the cookies out of the oven
    4. Let the cookies cool for 10 to 15 minutes
