Decepticon Review

These are my reviews/opinions of some of the main decepticons of the Transformers Generation One series. This show was from 1984 to 1987.

Shockwave: 8/10

Shockwave is the main scientist for the decepticons. He is very loyal to Megatron, but he is very unusual when it comes to his experiments. He is brutal and will not let anyone try to stop the Decepticons.

Starscream: 9/10

Starscream is the leader of the seekers. He is very smart, but he always tries to overthrow Megatron any chance he gets. He is also a traitor to the decepticons on multiple occasions.

Megatron:9/10

Megatron is the leader of the decepticons and rivial of autobot leader Optimus Prime. He is a great fighter, but he gets very angry and is usually very arrogant.

Ravage: 8/10

Ravage is one of Soundwave's cassette bots. Even though he does not speak, he speaks his loyalty to the Decepticon cause in his actions.

Soundwave:10/10

Soundwave is the Decepticon Communications Officer. He is stoic and often silent, but he has extreme work ethic and will get the job done.
